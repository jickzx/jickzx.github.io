Query a Degree

    May 1, 2025

    Query a Degree took in your predicted grades for A Levels and current grades for GCSEs. Then, it compared them to previous students' grades and the courses they got into, and what university they went to. Then, it showed you the chance of you getting into that specific course at that university.

    I also made sure to also add a section for Degree Apprenticeships, as some students would also apply for that too.
